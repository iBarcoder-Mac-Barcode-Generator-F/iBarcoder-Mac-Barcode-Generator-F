## About iBarcoder for Mac

**iBarcoder Mac** is a powerful application for creating barcodes and QR codes directly on your Mac. Tailored for macOS, it delivers a smooth, intuitive experience for professionals managing retail, logistics, inventory, and asset tracking.

It supports a wide range of barcode types, including:  

- **QR Code**  
- **Code 128**  
- **EAN-13 & UPC-A**  
- **Data Matrix**  
- **PDF417**  
- **Aztec**  

Users can fully customize barcodes — including content, size, colors, and text labels. Batch generation allows producing multiple codes simultaneously, while exports are available in **vector formats** (PDF, EPS, SVG) for printing and **raster formats** (PNG, JPEG) for digital use.

Optimized for **macOS Monterey, Ventura, and Sonoma**, iBarcoder runs seamlessly on both **Intel Macs** and **Apple Silicon (M1/M2/M3)**, ensuring fast and reliable performance. Its native interface lets users generate accurate, scannable barcodes quickly and efficiently.
